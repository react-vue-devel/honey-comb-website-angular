Why Users Love Honeycomb

Unrestrictive, real-value reward tokens

01.

Unrestrictive loyalty rewards with a real value

Brands & retailers on Honeycomb reward you with Honeycomb’s native crypto token HONEY (HNE). With HNE tokens you’re in control - redeem for purchases with any brand or retailer on the Honeycomb network, transfer or gift HNE tokens to friends and family, or sell your HNE tokens for cash on any crypto exchange

02.

Get access to coupons, special offers & more!

Honeycomb isn’t simply an unrestrictive loyalty program! With Honeycomb you can receive specialized offers and coupons for completing brand surveys, social media engagement, and, completing special tasks. Honeycomb’s mobile app will keep you in the loop on the latest and greatest offers.

03.

Zero fees, no expiry

Want to send your HNE tokens to a friend in Japan for her birthday? Do it - and with zero fees. Want to save up tokens you earn from daily food purchases for that next big trip? Do it - HNE tokens never expire! The only time you’ll pay (a very minimal fee!) is when you convert HNE tokens to cash.
